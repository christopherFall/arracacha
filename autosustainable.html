Arracacia Xanthorrhiza Bancroft
Arracacha is a tuber that has gained interest in self-sustainability projects due to its ability to adapt to diverse growing conditions, its low environmental impact and its nutritional value.
  • Low enviromental impact.
  • Short and efficient life cycle.
  • Resistance to pests.
  • Promotion of agrotourism
  • Support for small farmers.
  • Dietary diversification and food resilience.
